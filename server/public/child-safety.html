Child Safety

Effective Date: 01/01/2025

Definitions and Scope

This policy applies to all users of BeHappyTalk and establishes strict guidelines to prevent child abuse, exploitation, and harm. For the purposes of this document:

  • Child: Any individual under the age of 18.
  • Abuse: Physical, emotional, or sexual mistreatment of a child.
  • Exploitation: The use of a child for personal, financial, or other gain.
  • Harm: Any action that negatively impacts a child's physical, emotional, or mental well-being.

Age Restrictions

  • BeHappyTalk is strictly for users aged 18 and above.
  • Accounts suspected of being operated by minors will be suspended pending verification.

Prohibited Conduct

  • Engaging in any form of communication, content sharing, or solicitation involving minors.
  • Grooming, coercion, or any attempt to exploit children in any way.
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting explicit content involving minors.
  • Any conduct that facilitates child exploitation, whether intentional or unintentional.

User Verification and Reporting

  • All users are required to complete a secure identity verification process.
  • Suspicious behavior should be reported through the in-app reporting system.
  • Reports will be reviewed promptly, and necessary actions, including law enforcement notification, will be taken.

Data Protection and Privacy

Any report involving minors will be handled with strict confidentiality while ensuring legal compliance.

Law Enforcement Collaboration

  • BeHappyTalk cooperates fully with law enforcement agencies in investigations of child exploitation.
  • Data and evidence related to potential violations will be provided to legal authorities as required.

Penalties and Enforcement

  • Any user found violating child safety policies will face immediate account suspension and a permanent ban.
  • Cases involving child exploitation will be reported to law enforcement.
  • Legal actions may be pursued against violators as per international law.

Education and Awareness

Regular updates will be made to strengthen security measures and enhance user awareness. This policy is enforceable worldwide and will be updated as necessary to comply with evolving international laws and best practices.

By using BeHappyTalk, users agree to abide by this Child Safety Policy. Violations will be taken seriously and addressed immediately.
